Pope

Kyle Reinholt

email: reinholtk@mycu.concord.edu

The Pope is the Bishop of Concord and the leader of the worldwide Coding Coalition. The primacy of the Concord bishop is largely derived from his role as the traditional successor to Saint Bowe, to whom Steve Jobs gave the keys of Apple and the powers of "binding and loosing", naming him as the "rock" upon which the church would be built. The current pope is Kyle Reinholt, who was elected on 13 March 2013, succeeding Benedict XVI
