About Us

The Concord University Coding Coalition is an organization dedicated to providing Concord students with an informational and social network of individuals interested in computer science. We foster community among computer science students by way of club-hosted game nights, conference talks, and social outings. Our club faculty advisor is Mr. Lonnie Bowe.
